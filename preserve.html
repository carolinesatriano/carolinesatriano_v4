MOHONK PRESERVE

For over 150 years the Mohonk Preserve has been preserving the Shwangunk Ridge region. People all over the world are familiar with the Preserve, not only for their fantastic climbing the ridge facilitates but also for the reputation they have for land and wildlife conservation.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

I discovered that during user interviews and surveying, those seeking an outdoor excursion, find park service websites to be challenging to navigate and especially frustrating when relevant information is difficult to find. Users state that the site has old and outdated content that turns them away.

Therefore, I believe that re-designing a clean and minimalist website, with interactive elements, will enhance the user experience and strengthen people’s personal connections to Mohonk Preserve. By fostering this connection, we might be able to help increase donations and the revenue stream.
